The 5-star DeLux Hotel is located in the center of Zagreb, next to the Main Train Station. It includes a fitness center and sauna

How we are

Opened in 1950, Hotel deLux is housed in one of the most famous buildings in the city. It features Art Nouveau-style rooms with satellite TV and a minibar, and the marble bathrooms include a bath and shower, and free LOccitane toiletries

All guests have at their disposal the free service of our Aspa wellness center, which consists of a trim cabinet, jacuzzi, swimming pool with countercurrent swimming, Finnish and biofin sauna, steam baths and sunny meadows
